What is Compounding Buddy?

Compounding Buddy is a quick reference for extemporaneous oral solution preparation — designed for pharmacists and pharmacy technicians who need a literature-cited recipe at the bench. Each entry includes ingredients, a step-by-step method, beyond-use dating, and a primary citation.

What Compounding Buddy is NOT

Compounding Buddy is not a substitute for Trissel's, King Guide, your institution's formulary, or a formal stability study. It does not replace pharmacist verification or account for patient-specific factors, institutional protocols, or local water quality considerations.

Data sources

Recipes are compiled from published stability studies in peer-reviewed pharmacy literature (primarily Am J Health-Syst Pharm and Ann Pharmacother). Each entry includes its primary citation. Consult the original article for full methodology and confidence intervals.

Coverage

Current version covers common oral solutions only, starting with formulations frequently needed in pediatric and adult care settings. Additional vehicles, concentrations, and dosage forms are planned for future versions.
